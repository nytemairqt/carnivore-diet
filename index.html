About This Topic

The carnivore diet is an all-meat, animal-based way of eating that eliminates plant foods entirely. Adherents report improvements in gut health, skin conditions, mental clarity, energy levels, and more. This site curates the best information from doctors, researchers, and community experience — no fluff, no dogma, just useful summaries and resources.

Not medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making dietary changes.
