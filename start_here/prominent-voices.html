Prominent Voices

The doctors, researchers, and community leaders shaping the carnivore movement.

One of the best things about the carnivore community is how much free information is out there. Real doctors, researchers, and everyday people sharing what works. Heres a list of voices worth paying attention to.

Anthony Chaffee MD
Former professional rugby player turned doctor. Chaffee is one of the most vocal advocates for a zero-plant diet. He breaks down the biochemistry of plant toxins and argues that plants are actively harmful to human health. His interviews with other carnivore doctors are some of the best in the space.
Shawn Baker MD
The godfather of the modern carnivore movement. Former orthopedic surgeon, world-record holding athlete. His book "The Carnivore Diet" is the foundational text. His YouTube channel features success stories, interviews, and Q&As. If theres one person who represents this way of eating, its Baker.
Bella (Steak & Butter Gal)
One of the biggest carnivore influencers on social media. Bella shares her journey of healing gut issues, skin problems, and hormonal imbalances through a beef, butter, and eggs template. Shes relatable, practical, and great for beginners who want to see what a normal carnivore day looks like.
Professor Bart Kay
A PhD in physiology who applies hard science to the nutrition debate. Kay is unabashedly pro-carnivore and anti-vegan. He dismantles nutrition studies for a living and explains the physiology of low-carb and carnivore in a way that's both rigorous and entertaining. His rants are legendary.
Dr. Elizabeth Bright
A doctor who focuses on the hormonal and thyroid aspects of carnivore. Her work covers how animal-based diets affect female hormones, thyroid function, and autoimmune conditions. She brings a perspective thats often missing from the more male-dominated corners of the community.
Eddie Goeke
A former vegan who switched to carnivore and never looked back. Eddie's content focuses on fitness, body composition, and the practical side of eating this way. He debunks vegan nutrition claims with data and personal experience. Good for the fitness-focused crowd.
Kent Carnivore
A long-term carnivore who shares practical day-to-day content. What he eats, how he cooks it, how much it costs, how it affects his health. No fluff, no gatekeeping - just straight-up information from someone whos been doing this for years.
Carnivore Ray
Ray is a no-nonsense carnivore advocate who talks about meat sourcing, cooking techniques, and the lifestyle side of the diet. He's big on nose-to-tail eating, organ meats, and getting the most nutrient density out of every meal.
HomeSteadHow
A channel focused on the homesteading and self-sufficiency side of carnivore. How raises his own meat, butchers his own animals, and shows you how to source food outside the industrial system. If you want to move towards growing and raising your own food, this is the place.
Dr Abs MChD BChD
A dentist by training who found carnivore as a solution to his own health issues. Dr Abs talks about the oral health angle - how diet affects teeth, gums, and facial development. He also covers the metabolic and evolutionary arguments for an animal-based diet.

This list isnt exhaustive. The carnivore space is full of people sharing their stories and their expertise. If you find someone who resonates with you, dig into their content. Theres no shortage of free, high-quality information out there.
