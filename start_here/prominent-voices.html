Prominent Voices

The doctors, researchers, and community leaders shaping the carnivore movement.

One of the best things about the carnivore community is how much free information is out there. Real doctors, researchers, and everyday people sharing what works. Heres a list of voices worth paying attention to.

Anthony Chaffee MD
Former professional rugby player and neurosurgeon. Dr. Chaffee is one of the most vocal advocates for a zero-plant diet. He breaks down the biochemistry of plant toxins and argues that plants are actively harmful to human health.
Shawn Baker MD
The godfather of the modern carnivore movement. Former orthopedic surgeon, world-record holding athlete. His book "The Carnivore Diet" is the foundational text. His YouTube channel features success stories, interviews, and Q&As.
Bella (Steak & Butter Gal)
One of the biggest carnivore influencers on social media. Bella shares her journey of healing gut issues, skin problems, and hormonal imbalances through a beef, butter, and eggs template. She's relatable, practical and shares some great recipes for those looking for a bit more variety in their carnivore journey.
Professor Bart Kay
Retired research scientist turned farmer who applies his critical mind and scientific rigour to the nutrition debate. Bart Kay dismantles nutrition studies for a living and explains the physiology of low-carb and carnivore in a way that's both rigorous and entertaining. His rants are legendary.
Dr. Elizabeth Bright
A doctor who focuses on the hormonal and thyroid aspects of carnivore. Her work covers how animal-based diets affect female hormones, thyroid function, and autoimmune conditions. She brings a perspective thats often missing from the more male-dominated corners of the community.
Eddie Goeke
Another one for the scientific crowd. Eddie's content consists of technical and scientific breakdowns, case-by-case handling of nutritional claims and arguments for the benefits of a meat-heavy diet. His book "Contraindicated" is a popular read in the carnivore crowd.
Kent Carnivore
A prominent figure in the carnivore space who's name isn't actually Kent. Lee's journey to health started when he lost his colon in a surgery. His unique perspective (seeing the contents of his ostomy bag) gave him insight very few have about how readily (or not) humans actually digest fiber and various plant materials.
Carnivore Ray
For those looking for some quality, wholesome and lighthearted entertainment. Ray outlines his day-to-day in a series of short videos, exposing his honest journey (falling off), recipes he's trying and gives you a clear look into the daily life of a carnivore.
HomeSteadHow
Reversed his congestive heart failure, depression and dropped over 100lbs eating nothing but meat. Kerry is just one of thousands of stories of the extreme healing potential of a carnivore diet. He covers meal-prep, tips for new carnivores and an honest look at the day-to-day life.

This list isnt exhaustive. The carnivore space is full of people sharing their stories and their expertise. If you find someone who resonates with you, dig into their content. Theres no shortage of free, high-quality information out there.
