What's in This Section

These guides cover the fundamentals of the carnivore diet. If you're brand new, start with Carnivore 101 — it covers the basics of what to eat, why it works, and how to begin. Once you understand the foundations, the other guides dive deeper into specific topics.

The Adaptation Phase is especially important if you're planning to start — it covers exactly what to expect in the first 30 days and how to handle the transition.
